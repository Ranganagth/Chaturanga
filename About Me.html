I'm Ranganath V, an engineering student at the National Institute of Engineering, Mysore.

I warmly welcome you to try this chess game, designed to help you practice and train to become the best in chess. This project aims to create a practical and usable product that functions both as software and an educational tool. It is designed without restrictions and is based on my personal understanding of the game.

Key Features:

  • Unlimited Practice: You can practice an unlimited number of times, gaining more knowledge and improving your skills with each game.
  • Player vs. Computer Analysis: This project analyzes the player's approach against a computer opponent, offering a dynamic and open-ended model that encourages creative interaction.
  • Non-Judgmental Environment: This system won't judge or complain about numerous practice attempts, allowing you to train freely and improve at your own pace.
